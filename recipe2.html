Baked Ziti

This amazing pasta dish is great for any occasion. Whether you're with family or hosting a dinner party, it's always guarenteed to hit the spot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars of spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

    • Directions:

    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
    2. In a large skillet, brown onion and ground beef over medium heat. Add spaghetti sauce, and simmer 15 minutes.
    3. Preheat the over to 350 degrees F. Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer as follows: 1/2 of the ziti, Provolone cheese, sour cream, 1/2 sauce mixture, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese and remaining sauce mixture. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
    4. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheeses are melted.
